# Privacy Policy for ET Calculator

**Last Updated**: July 20, 2026

**ET Calculator** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the **ET Calculator** mobile application.

## 1. Information Collection and Storage
- **Offline Operations**: ET Calculator operates completely offline and does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data or usage analytics on external servers.
- **Local Storage**: All data created within the application (such as production records, machine selections, and count logs) is stored locally on your device using a local SQLite database (`production.db`).
- **File Export**: The app allows you to export your production summary data to Microsoft Excel (`.xlsx`) files. Exported files are created locally on your device and are shared only when you explicitly choose to share them using your device's native sharing menu.

## 2. Permissions
ET Calculator requires minimal permissions to function:
- **Storage Access**: Required only to save and export Excel files locally to your device storage or shared cache.

## 3. Third-Party Services & Tracking
- ET Calculator does **not** integrate third-party advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or remote analytics frameworks.
- No personal information is transmitted to third parties.
